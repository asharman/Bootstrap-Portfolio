About Me

Hi my name is Austin!
I'm a Web Developer who is attending UCF's Web Development Boot Camp!
I got into programming because of the rapid growth in the industry, there is always something new to learn and an infinite way to apply these concepts.
Before I joined the Boot Camp I worked in the specialty coffee industry. I'm obsessed with all of the details that impact floavor in a cup of coffee. From where it was grown to how it gets roasted and brewed all change how the final cup tastes when it gets to your hands. I enjoy showing people that there is more than meets the eye on the simple cup of coffee that they drink everyday.
When I'm not drinking programming or thinking about coffee I'm usually planning my next trip. My partner and I love exploring the world and meeting new people. Our last trip was to Peru! We hiked up Macchu Picchu and visited the oasis in Huacachina.
